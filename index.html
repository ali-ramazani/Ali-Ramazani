About Me
As the world becomes increasingly digital, the demand for skilled computer scientists has never increased. As a dedicated student of Computer Science at Berea College, located in Kentucky, USA, I am passionate about technology and innovation. I concomitantly seek to expand my knowledge and skills in this exciting field. My journey in programming began after graduating from high school when I started to teach myself programming. Since then, I have been captivated by the art of building visually appealing dynamic websites that make the user experience as pleasant as possible. The first programming language I learned was C++, followed by HTML and CSS. I have taken several classes that have equipped me with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in this domain, including Software Design & Implementation, Data Structures & Algorithms, Discrete Mathematics, and Calculus. In addition to my technical skills, I possess exceptional communication abilities, a keen eye for detail, and strong leadership skills, which were further honed in my capacity as an ESL teacher for 4 years. Managing 10 ESL classes daily and meeting deadlines has also helped me develop a strong work ethic and the ability to handle pressure and an increasing workload. Proficient in Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and C++, I am excited to apply my diverse skillset and explore various opportunities in the tech industry. I am well-suited to multiple roles, from software developer to web designer. I look forward to using my knowledge and experience to drive innovation and push the boundaries of what is possible.
One of my hobbies is playing chess, so if you'd love to play a game with me, feel free to contact me at ramazani2020 on chess.com
